How Much Does a Roof Cost in Boynton Beach, Florida? (2025 Guide)

Boynton Beach has one of the widest cost spreads of any city in Palm Beach County. Older neighborhoods, newer developments, and waterfront communities create a pricing spectrum that's broader than average.

Boynton Beach Roof Pricing (2025 Estimates)

Architectural Shingle

$12,500 – $28,000+

  • Budget-friendly
  • Popular west of Congress & Military Trail

Tile Roofs

$30,000 – $65,000+

  • Common east of Congress and in newer communities

Premium Clay Tile / Composite

$48,000 – $95,000+

Metal Roofs

$32,000 – $80,000+

Flat Roofs (Older homes & condos)

$15,000 – $70,000+

What Makes Boynton Unique?

  • Many roofs from the early 90s are now failing
  • Wood rot is more frequent here than nearby cities
  • Many communities restrict roofing colors or profiles
  • Flat roofs are common in older east-side neighborhoods
